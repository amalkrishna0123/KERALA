Mr Zaheesh

About the
project

Nestled on a 45’ X 70' plot, Mr Zaheesh’s home is a creative marvel, blending contemporary style with bold color accents and expansive open spaces. Exceptionally modern and stylish, the residence exudes a unique charm that reflects Mr Zaheesh’s vibrant personality.

Every aspect of the home has been thoughtfully designed to inspire and delight. From the striking exterior featuring sleek lines and eye-catching hues to the interior layout boasting seamless transitions and inviting ambiance, this home is a true reflection of modern living at its finest. One of the standout features of this abode is the innovative use of space, maximizing natural light and fostering a sense of openness throughout.

Design
Concept

The design concept for Mr Zaheesh’s modern home is centered around bold creativity, contemporary flair, and the seamless integration of open spaces. The architecture embodies a unique blend of clean, minimalist lines with vibrant color accents, creating a striking visual impact.

Emphasis is placed on maximizing natural light and fostering a sense of openness throughout the interior, enhancing the overall living experience. The interior design features an eclectic mix of modern furnishings, artistic elements, and luxurious finishes, creating a unique and inviting atmosphere.

Finishing &
Touches

The construction of Mr. Zaheesh’s home in Bangalore represents a remarkable blend of modern architecture and traditional design elements, culminating in a residence that is both aesthetically pleasing and functionally robust. One of the primary obstacles was the unpredictable nature of Bangalore's weather.

To combat this, the project team implemented contingency planning, including temporary coverings and adjusting work schedules to capitalize on favorable weather conditions. Bureaucratic procedures related to permits and local regulations posed another layer of complexity. To address this issue, effective communication with local authorities was essential, ensuring all paperwork was in order and facilitating a smoother workflow.
