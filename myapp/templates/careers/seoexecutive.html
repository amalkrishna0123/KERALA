SEO Executive

Company Name: RoyalLinks Constructions

Location: HSR Layout, Bangalore

Salary Range: ₹18,000 – ₹25,000 per month

Job Summary:

We are looking for a skilled and energetic SEO Executive with 1 year of experience to join our small but ambitious team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in SEO strategies, keyword research, and analytics, and be a proactive team player who is not shy in collaborating with others or presenting ideas. You’ll work closely with the team head to enhance our website’s search engine rankings, ensuring Royallinks reaches right audiences effectively.

Key Responsibilities:

  • ✅ Conduct keyword research and analysis to identify high-value search terms related to luxury dhotis, ethnic wear, and premium fashion for both Indian and international markets.
  • ✅ Optimize website content, including product descriptions, blogs, and landing pages, to improve organic search rankings on Google, Bing, and other search engines.
  • ✅ Implement on-page and off-page SEO strategies, including meta tags, URL structure, internal linking, and backlink building, targeting luxury and cultural niches.
  • ✅ Monitor and analyze website performance using tools like Google Analytics, Google Search Console, SEMrush, Ahrefs, or similar, providing monthly reports and recommendations.
  • ✅ Collaborate with the digital marketing team to align SEO efforts with social media, content marketing, and paid ad campaigns.
  • ✅ Stay updated on SEO trends, algorithm changes (e.g., Google updates), and best practices in the luxury fashion and e-commerce space.
  • ✅ Actively participate in brainstorming sessions, confidently pitching SEO ideas and contributing to team strategy.
  • ✅ Support the creation of high-quality, keyword-optimized content (in partnership with writers or the digital team) to boost brand authority and visibility.

Required Qualifications:

  • ✅ Experience: Minimum 1 years of company experience in SEO. Not including any course or internship.
  • ✅ Communication Skills: Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English.
  • ✅ Education: Bachelor’s degree

Preferred Skills:

  • ✅ Exceptional team player—collaborative, supportive, and willing to share ideas.
  • ✅ Outgoing and not shy—confident in presenting data-driven insights, engaging with stakeholders, and communicating with external partners.
