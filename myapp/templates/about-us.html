Behind The Build

Rooted in Tradition, Built for Generations

Kerala Home Interiors and Manufacturers is where timeless tradition meets contemporary living. Founded and led by expert engineer Ramesh P S, our firm specializes in crafting homes that beautifully blend the rich architectural heritage of Kerala with the comfort and functionality of modern design. With a deep respect for the intricate art of traditional Kerala architecture — from sloping tiled roofs to intricately carved woodwork — we infuse these timeless elements into homes tailored for today’s lifestyle. Every project is a celebration of culture, sustainability, and innovation. Whether you're dreaming of a serene nalukettu-style courtyard home or a chic, modern villa with heritage-inspired accents, we bring your vision to life with precision and passion. At Kerala Home Interiors and Manufacturers, we don’t just build homes — we craft soulful spaces that tell your story.
